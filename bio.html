About the Author

I am Anthony Wohlfeil, a senior studying computer engineering at the University of Michigan. I am in Ling 115 because I am very interested in learning languages and about their cultural/societal impact and it is part of my international minor. I also would like to learn more about natural language processing (NLP) so this kind of study is relevant to that field. For this class, my language contact assignment was Hawaiian Pidgin English and it has been a very interesting topic to research. One of my favorite parts about researching this language contact situation was discovering the existence of Da Jesus Book, a version of the New Testament of the Bible translated into Hawaiian Pidgin English.
